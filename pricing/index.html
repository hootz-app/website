FAQs

Got questions? Find answers here

What is Mezuto AI?

Mezuto is your friendly AI companion for Mac that helps you focus. Like a supportive friend, it blocks distractions, plays focus music, and gently nudges you to stay on track.

Who is Mezuto for?

Mezuto is for anyone who wants to in increase their focus and productivity - whether you're a student, writer, developer, designer, founder, or just someone who wants to get more done.

How does it block distractions?

When you start a focus session, Mezuto automatically blocks distracting apps and websites you've selected. It stays active until your session ends.

Can I customize which apps and sites to block?

Yes! You can create your own blocklist of apps and websites that distract you.

How does it track my productivity?

Mezuto gives you insights about your focus sessions, showing when you're most productive and how well you stay focused.

Is my data private?

Yes, your data is stored locally on your Mac and is never shared.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, you can try Mezuto free for 7 days with all features included.

What happens after the trial?

You can subscribe monthly or annually to continue using all features.
